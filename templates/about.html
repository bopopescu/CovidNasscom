About the application

The COVID-19 pandemic, also known as the coronavirus pandemic, is an ongoing pandemic of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID‑19), caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS‑CoV‑2).The outbreak was first identified in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.The World Health Organization declared the outbreak a Public Health Emergency of International Concern on 30 January, and a pandemic on 11 March.As of 21 May 2020, more than 5 million cases of COVID-19 have been reported in more than 188 countries and territories, resulting in more than 328,000 deaths. More than 1.89 million people have recovered from the virus.

The covid-19 tracker is a website which one can access to gain any knowledge about covid-19. The website has mentors and cousellors who can be contacted to gain information about covid-19 pandamic. The person using the website can choose a mentor and talk to him/her and clear all his doubts about covid and related issues. The main purpose of the website is to spred awareness.
